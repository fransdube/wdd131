Site Plan: Hiking Trails in Gauteng

Site Name

Hiking Trails in Gauteng

This name represents a website dedicated to showcasing popular hiking trails in Gauteng, South Africa. It is simple, descriptive, and relevant to the target audience.

Optional Domain: hikingtrailsgauteng.co.za

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a comprehensive resource for hikers in Gauteng. It will include:

  • Detailed information about popular hiking trails, including difficulty, length, and highlights.
  • Photos and maps for each trail.
  • Tips for hikers, such as what to bring and safety guidelines.
  • A contact form for visitors to ask questions or share their experiences.

Scenarios

Here are two scenarios that represent the target audience of the site:

  1. What are the best hiking trails for beginners in Gauteng?
  2. Where can I find detailed maps and directions to the trails?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used in the website:

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - Used for headers, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #333 (Dark Gray) - Used for text and backgrounds.
  • Accent Color: #FFC107 (Yellow) - Used for hover effects and highlights.

Typography

The following fonts will be used in the website:

  • Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Bold and modern for headings.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Clean and readable for paragraphs.
